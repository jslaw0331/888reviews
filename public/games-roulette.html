Popular roulette online game variations

Online roulette offers multiple thrilling formats, each with unique rules and odds. Understanding these core variations is key to choosing the game that best fits your strategy.

European Roulette

Featuring a wheel with a single zero, European Roulette offers the best standard odds for players. The single green zero reduces the house edge to 2.70%, making it the most player-friendly version globally.

American Roulette

Identified by its double zero (0 and 00) layout, American Roulette increases the house edge to 5.26%. This extra green pocket alters probability, giving the casino a significantly greater advantage.

French Roulette

Similar to European Roulette with a single zero, French Roulette is renowned for player-friendly La Partage and En Prison rules on even-money bets, effectively halving the house edge to 1.35% when the ball lands on zero.

Specialty games

This category includes innovative variants like Lightning Roulette with random multiplier prizes, and Multi-Wheel Roulette allowing simultaneous bets on up to eight wheels for substantially higher payouts.

Online roulette for beginners: how to play your first game

Roulette may look complex, but it is one of the easiest casino games to learn. In just a few minutes you will understand the basics and be ready to place your first bet.

The objective: it’s all about prediction

The goal is beautifully simple: predict which numbered pocket the ball will land in after the wheel is spun. Place chips on the corresponding section of the betting table — if the ball lands on your bet, you win.

Understanding the roulette table: wheel & mat

The game has two main parts: the wheel (37 or 38 numbered pockets) and the betting mat where you place chips. The mat mirrors the wheel’s numbers and offers additional betting areas for groups of numbers.
