Behind the reels: slot game mechanics explained

Understanding a few key terms can dramatically improve your slot gaming experience and help you choose the right games.

Return to Player (RTP)

RTP is the theoretical percentage of all wagered money a slot pays back to players over time. For example, a slot with 96% RTP will, on average, return RM96 for every RM100 wagered.

Volatility (or variance)

Low volatility slots offer frequent but smaller wins — great for longer sessions. High volatility slots pay out less often but have the potential for much larger wins.

Paylines & Megaways

Paylines are fixed patterns across the reels that winning combinations must land on. Megaways randomizes symbols on each reel, creating thousands of potential ways to win on every spin.
