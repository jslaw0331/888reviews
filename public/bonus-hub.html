Bonus terms and conditions: the fine print explained

The secret to successfully using a casino bonus lies in understanding its terms and conditions. This section demystifies the most important terms in simple, clear language.

Wagering requirements (playthrough)
The number of times you must wager bonus funds before they become withdrawable cash. For example, a RM100 bonus with x35 playthrough means RM3,500 in total bets.
Minimum deposit
The smallest amount you must deposit to claim a bonus — often around RM50—RM100 for Malaysian players.
Game weighting & restrictions
Different games contribute different percentages toward wagering. Slots usually contribute 100%; table games may contribute 10% or nothing at all.
Validity period / time limits
The timeframe to use your bonus and meet wagering — from 24 hours for some free spins to 30 days for match bonuses.
Max bet & win caps
The highest stake allowed per spin or hand while a bonus is active, and any maximum withdrawable amount from bonus winnings.

A wagering (or rollover) requirement specifies how many times you must bet the bonus — or bonus + deposit — before any winnings become withdrawable. A 35× requirement on a MYR 100 bonus means you must wager MYR 3,500 before withdrawing. Lower multipliers are generally easier to clear, but game weighting and time limits still apply.
