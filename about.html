About 888reviews

We publish independent online casino reviews, bonus analysis, and editorial guides so you can compare licensed operators with clarity, not slogans. We are not a casino and we do not take bets; we explain how products work, what terms matter, and where to verify facts before you play.

Our mission

Online gambling moves fast: new brands, shifting rules, and bonus headlines that rarely tell the full story. 888reviews exists to slow that down just enough, so you get structured benchmarks, plain-language explainers, and sources you can double-check. We focus on casino reviews, bonus comparisons, slots and providers, how-to guides, and industry news that helps you understand the market, not chase it.

Editorial standards

Every review and guide is written for readers first. We cite licensing and public compliance signals where we can, explain wagering and game eligibility in bonus offers, and call out trade-offs: speed versus limits, breadth versus depth, headline value versus realisable value. When we are uncertain or data is incomplete, we say so. Offers, terms, and eligibility change; always confirm on the operator’s site and with regulators in your region.

Independence & transparency

888reviews is an editorial publication. We may earn commissions when you use links to third-party operators; where required, we disclose commercial relationships. Compensation does not buy a positive review or a fixed score. Our pages are built to explain why we recommend or caution against a product. If you believe we have missed something important, we want the facts to be right: use official regulator registers and each operator’s terms as the final word.

Who we serve

Our readers are adults 18+ who can legally gamble online where they live. Territory rules differ; we do not guarantee that any operator accepts players from your location. Nothing here is legal, tax, or investment advice. If you are unsure about legality, stop and seek qualified advice or use official government and regulator resources.

Responsible gambling

Gambling is entertainment with a risk of loss. We encourage limits, breaks, and self-exclusion tools where operators offer them. If gambling stops being fun, seek help: national helplines and organisations such as BeGambleAware (UK) or local equivalents can support you. Play responsibly.
